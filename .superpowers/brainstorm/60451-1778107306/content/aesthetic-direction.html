D — Cozy Pixel / Lo-fi Aesthetic

Warm, lo-fi vibes with soft pixel art. Think cozy room with a bookshelf and desk lit by a lamp. Softer palette, charming character sprites, ambient animation (rain, candle flicker). Approachable and warm.
