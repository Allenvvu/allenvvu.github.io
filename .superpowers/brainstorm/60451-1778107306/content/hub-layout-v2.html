How it works

  • Full-viewport <canvas> runs the pixel agent simulation as a live background
  • Agents are tiny colored squares (1–2px) that wander the grid with simple movement rules
  • They leave a fading trail — dark fade gives depth without being distracting
  • Your name + two room buttons float as a semi-transparent overlay on top
  • Sub-pages (bookshelf, gallery) drop the canvas and use a simple dark pixel aesthetic
